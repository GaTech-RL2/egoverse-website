Mecka App

iPhone-based capture pipeline

How it works

  1. Open the Mecka app and sign in with your work email
  2. Confirm you are in the correct organization
  3. Tap "+ New" to start a recording
  4. Frame the task clearly and follow the on-screen prompts
  5. Perform the task, then tap "Stop" when finished
  6. Review the recording
  7. Fill in the required details
  8. Tap Save
  9. Upload the recording and wait for it to complete
  10. Open the Mecka partner web app (partners.mecka.ai), sign in with the same account, and confirm the recording appears there

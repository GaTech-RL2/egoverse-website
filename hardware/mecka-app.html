Mecka App

iPhone-based capture pipeline for community-accessible EgoVerse recording.

How it works

  1. Mount an iPhone on a head strap and record ultrawide egocentric RGB at 1080p/30fps.
  2. Upload to the processing service.
  3. Pipeline estimates head pose (6-DoF) and 3D hand pose (21 keypoints per hand).
  4. Outputs are converted into the unified EgoVerse format for browsing and training.

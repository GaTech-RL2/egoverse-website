EgoVerseEgocentric human data from around the world, built for robot learning.

EgoVerse is an ecosystem for curating, accessing, and learning from egocentric human data for robot learning. It is a "living" dataset, continuously expanded by the consortium, and driven by a research community advancing rigorous studies of human-to-robot transfer across tasks and embodiments.
